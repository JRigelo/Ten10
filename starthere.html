Start Here

Ever heard of Pay it Forward?

How about Random Acts of Kindness?

Well we’re taking the good deed ideal to another level.

Think of it as friends helping friends supporting each other to give something back to the world.

Choose Ten Causes

To be done within one year, get involved and pick some things that you care about. It can be as big as organizing a park clean up or as small as giving a book you’ve read to someone you care about.

Set Some Deadlines

You have the whole year to complete the list after you fleshed out your ideas. Set some soft and hard deadlines for yourself based on the action items within your list.

Pick a Friend

You will receive continued support from your friend that suggested The Ten10 Experiment to you. If you decided to begin this on your own, make sure to choose at least 1 friend that can check in on you achieving each goal.

Tell 10 Friends

Share what you will accomplish with 10 of your friends. Most likely they’d want to join you too! Light that spark so they can chose their own Ten10.

Are you ready to be part of something epic?

Let’s change the world together.
