THE EXPERIMENT

If you are familiar with “Pay it forward” and “Random Acts of Kindness”, this is a similar idea. It just takes it a step further - getting your friends involved to keep each other accountable. Well we’re taking the good deed ideal to another level.

Choose Ten Causes

to be done within one year. Determine some causes you are passionate about and get involved. It can be as big as organizing a park clean up or as small as giving a book that inspired you to a stranger.

Set Some Deadlines

you have the whole year to complete the list after fleshing out your ideas. Set some soft and hard deadlines based on the action items within your 10 list.

Pick a Friend

you will receive continued support from your friend that suggested The Ten10 Experiment to you. If you decided to begin this on your own, make sure to choose at least 1 friend that can check in with you periodically.

Tell 10 Friends

Share what you aspire to accomplish with 10 of your friends. Most likely they’d want to join you too! Light that spark so they can choose their own Ten10; provide them with support, discuss their ideas, and give feedback to help them stay motivated.

Are you ready to be part of something epic?

Let’s change the world together.
